Apple Inc. — Form 10-K (Fiscal Year 2023) — Selected Excerpts

Item 1. Business
The Company designs, manufactures and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables and accessories, and sells a variety of related services. The Company's products include iPhone, Mac, iPad, and Wearables, Home and Accessories. iPhone remains the Company's largest product category by net sales.

Item 1A. Risk Factors
The Company's business, results of operations and financial condition are subject to numerous risks. Global and regional economic conditions could materially adversely affect the Company. The Company is exposed to the risk of write-downs on the value of its inventory. The Company depends on component and product manufacturing and logistical services provided by outsourcing partners, many of which are located outside of the United States, creating concentration and geopolitical risk. Future operating results depend upon the Company's ability to obtain components in sufficient quantities on commercially reasonable terms. The Company faces substantial competition characterized by aggressive price competition and rapid technological advances.

Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis
Total net sales decreased 3 percent during fiscal 2023 compared to 2022, driven primarily by lower net sales of Mac and iPhone, partially offset by higher net sales of Services. Services net sales increased 9 percent during 2023, reaching a new all-time high. Gross margin percentage increased to 44.1 percent during 2023 compared to 43.3 percent in 2022, driven by cost savings and a higher mix of Services. The Company returned over 90 billion dollars to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases during the fiscal year.
